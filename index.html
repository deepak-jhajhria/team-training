For teachers

Starting from the long-term vision of the Documentation Center of Cambodia in guiding Cambodian ships towards the path of peace, reconciliation and prevention Genocide A program to research and educate about genocide in Cambodia, especially the history of the Khmer Rouge, established in 2004. Through some important work before reaching the training of general social science teachers on how to teach the history of Democratic Kampuchea (1975) - (1979).

National Trainer

These include:

- Compiling historical documents related to the Khmer Rouge regime

- Writing books on the history of Democratic Kampuchea

- Reviewing and revising the writings of experts and the Royal Government of Cambodia

- Compiling instruction manuals for teachers on teaching the history of Democratic Kampuchea Incorporation of the curriculum from the history of the Khmer Rouge into the secondary and high school curriculum of the Ministry of Education, Youth and Sports, as well as the inspection of teachers' teaching to students.
